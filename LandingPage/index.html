About Us

At Coffee Shop, we believe that coffee is more than just a beverage; it's a way of life. Our journey began in 1995 when Ritche Mohan had a vision to create a coffee haven where people could come together, experience exceptional coffee, and forge lasting connections. Over the years, we've grown from a small corner cafe into a thriving community hub, all while staying true to our commitment to quality and sustainability.
